Our story

Built by a contractor.
For everyone the old
platforms forgot.

We got tired of watching homeowners get overcharged and contractors get squeezed. So we built something different.

BuiltNotBroken started on job sites in Calgary NW. Evan Thompson, owner of ET's Quality Stucco & Stone Repairs, spent years doing stucco and parging work across the city — real trades work, real clients, real results.

What he kept seeing bothered him. Homeowners calling for quotes that never came. Platforms charging contractors $300–$500 a month just to show up in search results. That cost going directly into every quote. Homeowners paying more without knowing why.

"The platform wasn't helping anyone. It was just taking a cut from both sides."

So he built BuiltNotBroken. A platform where homeowners post jobs for free, contractors pay only when they get a real lead, and nobody's markup is hiding inside your quote.

We're Calgary-founded, community-driven, and we're not interested in being the next HomeStars. We're interested in being what HomeStars should have been — a fair marketplace where the best contractor wins, not the one who spent the most on advertising.

We're early. We're growing. And every job posted on this platform is a vote for a fairer way to hire a contractor in this city.
